About US

Topic

The topic of our group is implementing a search engine for the entertainment industry of Taiwan. When people are searching for stage name like “熱狗”,”蛋堡”,”胡瓜”, Google often return contents about food or something else that we are not looking for. Otherwise, people must spend extra time adding keyword such as “藝人”,”歌手”,”主持人”,”演員” and so on. In view of this, we want to implement a search engine that can help users search for information, news or pictures about artists in a more efficient and accurate way.
